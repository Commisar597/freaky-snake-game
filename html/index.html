The game starts by left-clicking on the „Play” button on the screen while on the game website and it drops into the game. There will be a grid of cells with the snake in it. The snake moves by itself in the direction it’s head is facing. The snake's head is oriented by using the arrow keys, or WASD on the keyboard. There will appear items on the grid which, when collided with, will add or remove from your overall score. You have 1:30 minutes to earn as much points as you can get. The game stops if the snake hits the borders or go into itslef. At the end you can see the points you have earned and the highest score is saved.
