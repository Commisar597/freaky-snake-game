The game starts by left-clicking on the „Play” button. There will be a grid of cells with a snake in it, this snake is moving on its own, and you can only orient its movement using WASD on your keyboard. A red object representing a fruit will appear randomly on this grid, when collided with your overall score will go up by 1, and your snake will grow by 1. You have 90 seconds to earn as many points as possible. The game stops if the snake hits the borders, collides with part of its body, or if the timer runs out. At the end you can see the points you have earned and your highest score will be saved.
